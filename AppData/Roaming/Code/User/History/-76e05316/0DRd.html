Karate Programs

Our Karate Programs are designed to help students of all ages and skill levels reach their full potential.
Our expert instructors are dedicated to helping every student develop the focus, discipline, and confidence to succeed.
Join us and discover how we empower every student to achieve their full potential, one kick at a time.

Kinder Karate (Ages 3-6)

Kinder Karate is great for improving social skills in young children, as well as improving their balance, coordination, and control. The major goals of this program are to teach focus, listening skills, and respect. This program is designed for students aged 3 to 6.
The goal of the program is to introduce young students to the benefits of the martial arts.
Students will work to develop and improve through the teaching of the basic karate techniques. Along with the physical development,
students will develop better concentration, focus, and listening skills.
The program will also teach children some basic street-smart techniques.

Kids Karate (Ages 7-12)

This program teaches students the basic martial arts and self-defense techniques, including blocks, punches, kicks, and stances. It is a program that allows us to teach the student how to be martial artists, preparing them on how to eventually become a black belt.
Our children’s programs are based on the core values of “Martial Arts begins and ends with Respect.” From this basic premise, we develop your child’s awareness and skills.

Adult Karate (Ages 13+)

We have blended the benefits of traditional martial arts with modern fitness and self-defense techniques. Students of all ages will discover the ultimate workout for the body and mind. Those who start our karate classes quickly see improvements in strength, stamina, flexibility, coordination, and overall physical condition.
Our classes are designed to give you an excellent cardiovascular workout, which allows you to strengthen the heart and burn calories. This provides the added benefit of exercising with a purpose!

We know that Martial Arts can change your life or your child’s life. We know that we can find the right program for you or your child that will help you reach your goals for improved fitness, better focus, discipline, and confidence.
