Kinder Karate is great for improving social skills in young children, as well as improving their balance, coordination, and control. The major goals of this program are to teach focus, listening skills, and respect. This program is designed for students aged 3 to 6.
The goal of the program is to introduce young students to the benefits of the martial arts.
Students will work to develop and improve through the teaching of the basic karate techniques. Along with the physical development,
students will develop better concentration, focus, and listening skills.
The program will also teach children some basic street-smart techniques.
